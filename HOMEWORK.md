## Quezon City LYDO Overview

The Quezon City Youth Development Office (QCYDO) is the city office that formulates and implements programs and services for Quezon City's youth. Its work includes the QC Scholarship Program, youth leadership and livelihood programs, youth organization registration, and technical assistance for Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) planning documents. QCYDO also supports the Quezon City Local Youth Development Council (LYDC/QCYDC), which helps plan and coordinate youth development programs in the city.

## Summary

The official Quezon City sources show that QCYDO focuses on scholarship support, youth leadership, youth organization development, entrepreneurship, out-of-school youth support, SK planning assistance, and volunteerism. The main active or recently updated items are the QC Scholarship Program, BYOB-QCYEP, youth organization registration, and SK plan review services. Several completed events from 2024 to 2026 show QCYDO's work in youth entrepreneurship, scholarships, volunteerism, peace-building, youth organization training, and city service caravans. No clearly dated upcoming QCYDO event after May 13, 2026 was found in the official sources checked.
