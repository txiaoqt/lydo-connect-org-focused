# PASIG YOUTH ORGS

## What This File Is

This is a **Pasig-only, proof-backed list of youth organizations and youth-serving organizations** built from publicly accessible sources as of **April 7, 2026**.

I need to be precise here: I **cannot honestly guarantee** that this is every single youth organization in Pasig, because:

- the public YORP search does **not** expose a clean city-only filter
- some Pasig groups appear to live only on Facebook, private docs, or internal LYDO/PCYDO records
- some school-based or parish-based groups may exist publicly but not be indexed well

What I **can** say is:

- every entry below has at least **one public proof link**
- every entry below has a clear **Pasig basis**
- I excluded entries that looked stale, broken, adult-only, or too weakly tied to Pasig
- sector labels were added on **June 2, 2026**, based on the public sources and classifications already cited here

## Important Scope Choice

Because your panel kept asking **"Aside from SK, ano pa?"**, I treated this file as a list of **Pasig youth/youth-serving organizations other than barangay SK councils themselves**.

I still included some **school-based organizations** and **SSLGs** when they were publicly recognized by Pasig City or directly listed in YORP, because Pasig's own 2025 GKA YO/YSO slide included some of them.

For the **sector label**:

- **Public / government-linked** means the organization is attached to a public school, government program, or state-linked youth structure.
- **Private / non-government** means the organization is a non-government, community-based, church-based, volunteer, incorporated, or civic group. This does **not** mean the group is necessarily profit-seeking; most youth organizations in this list appear to be nonprofit or volunteer-oriented.
