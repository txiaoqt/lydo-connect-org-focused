# 3.2.2 Class Diagram

## Interpretation

- `AuthUser`, `UserProfile`, `Role`, `UserRole`, `AdminAccount`, `PolicyVersion`, and `UserPolicyAcceptance` represent identity, profile, access control, and policy agreement records.
- `Program`, `Event`, `Organization`, organization reference/project classes, and registration classes represent youth engagement, participation tracking, external registration sync status, and public organization information viewing with verified references.
- `DisclosureDocument`, `DocumentDownload`, barangay financial, youth metric, and compliance classes represent transparency and governance reporting.
- `CitizenTicket`, `TicketType`, `ServiceAdvisory`, and `AuditLog` support citizen services and accountability.
