# 3.8 Instrument Reliability and Ethics Clearance Support

This section is included because some schools or ethics reviewers require evidence that the evaluation instrument is valid and reliable. For LYDO Connect, this section should be framed as a **plan for instrument validation and reliability checking**, not as fabricated final results.

## 3.8.1 Content Validation

Before pilot testing, the evaluation questionnaire should be reviewed by qualified validators such as:

- the research adviser;
- a faculty member with systems evaluation experience;
- an IT practitioner or software expert; and
- if available, a domain stakeholder familiar with LYDO operations.

The validators should review whether the questionnaire items are clear, relevant, and aligned with the system's actual features.
They should also verify that items cover policy agreement behavior, registration and sync monitoring, source credibility checks, and null-safe handling of unavailable organization details.

## 3.8.2 Pilot Testing and Reliability Analysis

After expert validation, the questionnaire may be pilot-tested on a small group of respondents who are similar to the intended evaluators but are not part of the final respondent set. The pilot responses may then be used to compute **Cronbach's alpha** for the full instrument or for each major criterion.

### Suggested Reliability Interpretation

| Cronbach's Alpha | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 0.90 and above | Excellent internal consistency |
| 0.80 - 0.89 | Good internal consistency |
| 0.70 - 0.79 | Acceptable internal consistency |
| 0.60 - 0.69 | Questionable internal consistency |
| Below 0.60 | Revision needed |

If the reliability score is low, unclear or redundant items should be revised before the final evaluation.

## 3.8.3 Ethics and Data Privacy Support

The study should also support ethics clearance by showing that:

- respondents will be informed about the purpose of the evaluation;
- participation will be voluntary;
- collected responses will be used only for academic evaluation of the system;
- personally identifiable information will be minimized where possible;
- the system's own authenticated user flow presents Terms of Service and Privacy Policy content and records acceptance of the active policy version;
- registration records, policy acceptance records, and citizen ticket records will be treated as sensitive operational data during testing and presentation;
- public organization details are documented from credible official sources and not fabricated when unavailable;
- access to evaluation data will be limited to the researchers and authorized reviewers.

The template's wording about `Reliability Test Result Required for Ethics Clearance` should not be copied as if results already exist. For this study, it is more appropriate to keep this section as a validation and reliability plan, then update it later with actual pilot-test values if your school requires them.
