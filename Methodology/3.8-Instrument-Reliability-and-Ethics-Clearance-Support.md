# 3.8 Instrument Reliability and Ethics Clearance Support

This section should be treated as a plan for validation and reliability checking rather than as a claim that final scores already exist.

## 3.8.1 Content Validation

The questionnaire should be reviewed by the adviser and, if available, by a technical reviewer or evaluator who can confirm that the items match the current LYDO Connect workflow.

## 3.8.2 Pilot Testing and Reliability Analysis

After validation, the questionnaire may be pilot-tested with a small group similar to the target respondents. The pilot responses may be used to compute Cronbach's alpha.

## 3.8.3 Ethics and Data Privacy Support

The study should ensure that:
- participation is voluntary
- responses are used only for academic evaluation
- personally identifiable information is minimized where possible
- authenticated user access is protected by policy agreement and role-based routing
- uploaded files and records are treated as sensitive operational data
- public content and organization details are based on actual records and source links

Do not copy template wording that assumes a completed reliability result. This section should remain a validation and ethics-support plan for the current app workflow.
