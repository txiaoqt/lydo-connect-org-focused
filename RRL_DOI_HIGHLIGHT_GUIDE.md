# RRL DOI Highlight Guide (Citation-Focused)

This file lists what to highlight in each article for citation use, plus the best available link per DOI.

Notes:
- `Best link` uses direct PDF when resolvable; otherwise DOI landing page (open PDF may require institution access).
- Highlight targets are phrased as citation cues so you can quickly find the exact sentence in the paper.

## Theme A: System Quality and One-Stop e-Government

### 1) DOI: 10.1080/07421222.2003.11045748
- Citation use: Theoretical basis for measurable IS success dimensions.
- Highlight in paper:
1. The section that defines the updated IS success dimensions (system quality, information quality, service quality, use, user satisfaction, net benefits).
2. The part that explains causal relationships among those dimensions.

## Quick Copy-Paste Line for Method of Citation Extraction

For each DOI above, extract and cite from:
1. `Abstract` (problem + objective + main finding),
2. `Findings/Results` (empirical support),
3. `Conclusion/Implications` (policy/system design relevance),
so your Chapter 2 claims are evidence-based and directly tied to your system problem.
